##  Tips for Asking Questions

### Best Practices
1. **Be Specific**: Include PO numbers, product codes, or material numbers when available
2. **Use Natural Language**: The agent understands both Hebrew and English
3. **Ask Follow-ups**: You can ask clarifying questions based on the agent's responses
4. **Request Analysis**: Ask for recommendations, trends, and business insights

### Question Formats That Work Well
- Direct queries: "...?" (What...?)
- Quantitative questions: "...?" (How many...?)
- Status checks: "...?" (Is/Are...?)
- Requests: "转 ..." (Give me...)
- Comparisons: "砖..." (Compare...)
- Recommendations: " 爪..." (What is the recommendation...)

